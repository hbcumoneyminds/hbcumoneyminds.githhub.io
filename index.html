About

Find Out More About Us

Many HBCU students face unique financial challenges, including higher debt levels and less access to wealth building resources.

Mission

To provide HBCU students with the necessary financial education, resources, and connections to empower them to make informed financial decisions, build wealth, and overcome financial challenges

  • Vision

    To create a future where HBCU students are equipped with comprehensive financial literacy skills, enabling them to navigate and thrive in the financial landscape.

  • Objectives

    Empower HBCU students with financial literacy skills through workshops, stock market training and foster a connection with financial institutions to secure their future and break the cycle of financial disenfranchisement.
